Definition 8.4.1.

Let \(f\) be a function such that \(f^{(n)}(a)\) exists for every natural number \(n\text{.}\) The Taylor series for \(f\) centered at \(x=a\) is the series \(T_f(x)\) defined by
\begin{align*} T_f(x) =\mathstrut \amp \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(k)}(a)}{k!}(x-a)^k\\ =\mathstrut \amp f(a) + f'(a)(x-a) + \frac{f''(a)}{2!}(x-a)^2 + \cdots + \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n + \cdots\text{.} \end{align*}
